What?

Sometimes you want to share an URL with your friends, but it's just too short! Now, there is an easy solution.

To be redirected to a given url, prepend it with lengthened.link.

For example, to go to Google, you can use http://lengthened.link/google.com.

Feeling extra adventurous? Prepend lengthened.link multiple times! The url lengthener will happily redirect you multiple times.

Why?

In this world where everybody is obsessed with optimisation, from URL shorteners to TV dinners, sometimes we can feel like there's no time for us to de-stress. This is where lengthened.link comes in, letting you appreciate the little things in life. Feel like you're underutilising your internet connection? lengthened.link. The possibilities are endless.
